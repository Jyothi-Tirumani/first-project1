what are the benefits of EarPods compared to Airpods

AirPods are wireless, Bluetooth enabled headphones that provide between three and six hours of talk and listening time, adaptive EQ, spatial audio, and sweat and water resistance, depending on the model. EarPods are wired in-ear headphones that include a built-in remote to adjust the volume, video and music playback, and answer or end calls. The 3rd Generation AirPods and AirPods Pro feature Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode, which allows you to listen to music or content at a low volume while still being aware of your surroundings.

what are the benefits of Apple EarPods.

Apple's wired EarPods are inexpensive, have handy in line controls and clear microphone. They never run out of battery, and you don't have to worry about one earbud ever going missing because of the wire.

what to look for when Purchasing Apple EarPods

When buying Apple EarPods, consider whether you need a lightning connector or a 3.5 mm headphone plug. The lightening connector is meant to fit recent models of the iPhone, while the EarPods with the 3.5 mm headphone plug can fit other devices and older iPhones.
